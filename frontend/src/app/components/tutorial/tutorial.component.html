Tutorial


  1. Click on "Create an auction post" to create a new auction
  2. Search auctions by keywords in the search bar
  3. Filter out auctions according to your current preferences e.g. Category or Country
  4. Check the details by clicking on a picture of an active auction
  5. Place a bid by clicking on a bid button
